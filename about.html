Welcome to Global College

Global College of Engineering & Technology, Kadapa is a premier institution approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to JNTUA, Anantapur. Established with a vision to provide quality technical education, our college has grown to become one of the leading engineering colleges in the region.

Our 30-acre lush green campus on NH-40 boasts modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities. We are committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development of our students.

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in technical education and research, producing globally competent engineers and technocrats with ethical values.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality education through innovative teaching-learning methods
  • To foster research and innovation in cutting-edge technologies
  • To develop industry-ready professionals with strong ethical values
  • To promote entrepreneurship and leadership skills among students
