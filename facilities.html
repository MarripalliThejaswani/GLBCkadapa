Our Facilities

World-class infrastructure for quality education

Campus Facilities

Our 30-acre lush green campus on NH-40 provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic excellence and overall development of students.

Campus Infrastructure

  • 30 Acres lush green campus on NH-40
  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms
  • Modern seminar halls with audio-visual facilities
  • Administrative block with all necessary amenities
  • Separate blocks for each department
  • Beautifully landscaped gardens and open spaces

Laboratories

We have well-equipped laboratories for all departments with latest equipment and software:

  • Computer Labs with high-end systems and necessary software
  • Electronics & Communication Labs with advanced equipment
  • Mechanical Engineering Labs with modern machinery
  • Civil Engineering Labs with testing equipment
  • Electrical & Electronics Labs with power systems
  • Language Lab for communication skills development

Library

Our library is a treasure house of knowledge with extensive collection of resources:

  • Spacious reading halls with comfortable seating
  • Collection of over 20,000 books covering all disciplines
  • National and international journals and periodicals
  • E-learning materials and digital resources
  • Online access to e-journals and databases
  • Newspapers and magazines section
  • Book bank facility for students

Hostel

We provide comfortable and secure hostel accommodation for our students:

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls within the campus
  • Rooms with attached bathrooms
  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Common rooms with TV and recreational facilities
  • Indoor games facilities
  • Healthy and hygienic mess facility
  • Laundry facilities

Sports & Gymnasium

We encourage sports and physical fitness among our students:

  • Well-equipped gymnasium with modern equipment
  • Outdoor sports facilities for cricket, football, volleyball, etc.
  • Indoor games facilities for chess, carrom, table tennis, etc.
  • Athletics track and field events facilities
  • Annual sports meet and inter-college tournaments
  • Trained coaches for various sports

Other Facilities

Cafeteria

Security

Wi-Fi Campus

High-speed internet connectivity across the campus

Medical Facility

First aid and medical care facilities available on campus
