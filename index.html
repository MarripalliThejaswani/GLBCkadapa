Welcome to Global College

Global College of Engineering & Technology, Kadapa is a premier institution approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to JNTUA, Anantapur. Our 30-acre lush green campus on NH-40 boasts modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities.

We are committed to providing quality education with experienced faculty, industry exposure, and excellent placement opportunities to shape the future of our students.
