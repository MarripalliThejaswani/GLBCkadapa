Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech

  • Pass in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
  • Valid EAMCET rank
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in PCM (40% for reserved categories)

M.Tech

  • B.Tech/B.E in relevant discipline
  • Valid GATE/PGECET score
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in qualifying degree

MBA

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Valid ICET score
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in qualifying degree

Scholarships Available

We provide free tuition fee and hostel fee for BAPSer qualified SC/ST and BG students. Various other government scholarships are also available for eligible candidates.
