Admission Process

Global College of Engineering & Technology offers admission to various undergraduate and postgraduate programs through state and national level entrance examinations.

1

Check Eligibility

Verify that you meet the eligibility criteria for the program you wish to apply for.

2

Appear for Entrance Exam

Appear for the relevant entrance exam (EAMCET/ECET/PGECET/ICET).

3

Participate in Counseling

Participate in the counseling process conducted by the respective authority.

4

College Admission

Complete the admission formalities at the college after seat allotment.

How to Apply

For admissions to our programs, follow these steps:

  1. Check the eligibility criteria for the program you're interested in
  2. Register and appear for the relevant entrance examination
  3. Participate in the counseling process
  4. After seat allotment, report to the college with required documents
  5. Complete the admission formalities and fee payment

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We provide various scholarships and financial aid options to support deserving students:

  • Free tuition fee and hostel fee for BAPSer qualified SC/ST and BG students
  • Government scholarships for eligible candidates
  • Merit-based scholarships for top rankers in entrance exams
  • Fee concession for economically weaker students
  • Education loans assistance through bank tie-ups

For more details about scholarships, contact our admission office.
